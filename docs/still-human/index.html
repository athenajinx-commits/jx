The six faculties the exam measures

Not today's AI skills — prompting, fact-checking, delegation — the machines solved those themselves years ago. What the exam measures is what stays scarce when intelligence is free: the human interior. Six faculties, each one a muscle that the assisted life quietly stops exercising.

How the reckoning works

  1. Train — six drill games at Centaur Academy, one per faculty. Every answer comes with the reasoning, so wrong answers teach more than right ones.
  2. Test — 24 situational-judgment questions, about 12 minutes. No trivia, no trick math: only judgment calls from real collaboration situations.
  3. Score — your AI-CQ lands on a 55–145 scale (like an IQ or EQ score, where 100 = working fluency). You get a skill-by-skill profile and targeted advice, not just a number.
🔮 The Oracle's counsel: at 100+ you are ready for the work reserved for humans. Below 100, the salary keeps you comfortable — but the good problems go to the ready. Nothing here is official; the Oracle only reads. Retake whenever you like. The Oracle is proud of you anyway.
