❓ How to Use This App

🗺️ Building your trip

  • ➕ Add Location creates a new stop. Click a stop to open it and fill in the place name, arrival date, departure date, and hotel.
  • Move a stop by pressing and dragging it (works with mouse or finger on touch screens) to anywhere on the board.
  • Connect two stops with an arrow to show your route: open a stop and click + Next (or + Alt for an alternative route), or click 🔗 Connect and then click the destination stop.
  • Remove a stop by opening it and clicking Delete. Remove just a connecting line by clicking the small red × next to its label.

💾 Saving and sharing your trip

  • 💾 Save My Trip — saves your work as a project file (JSON format) on your device so you can close the browser and continue later. Use 📂 Open Saved Trip to load that same JSON file back in and pick up where you left off.
  • 📝 Download Itinerary (Markdown) — a simple text file you can take with you and read anywhere, even without internet, on your phone, in an email, or in any notes app.
  • 🖼️ Download Itinerary (Picture) — a snapshot image of your trip map, handy for sharing on chat apps or social media.
  • 📄 Download Itinerary (PDF) — a printable document version of your trip map, good for printing or attaching to an email.

🌙 Dark Mode

Use the Dark Mode button in the top-right to switch between light and dark colors — your choice is remembered next time you visit.

You can reopen these instructions anytime with the ❓ Help button.
